Instructions

Welcome to JSTanks.

You will be controlling a tank in this game. There will be a Homebase located at the bottom of the map which you have to protect from enemy tanks trying to destroy it. You will have to destroy all enemy tanks in order to win.

Your tank will be damaged when hit with enemy shots. Multiple hits from the enemy will destroy your tank and the game will be over.

The map will include brick walls and steel walls. Brick walls can be destroyed by firing at them once. Steel walls are stronger than brick walls and require multiple shots to be destroyed.

Good luck and enjoy!

Controls:

Use Arrow keys to move around the map and press the 'F' key to fire.
